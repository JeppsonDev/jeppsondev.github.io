Description

SLOPETRON is a thrilling cyberpunk arcade bike racing game, with simplistic controls and gameplay that can be played over and over to master the procedurally generated levels! Super easy to pick up and learn, but difficult to master

History

Slopetron began as a project in late July where the goal was to create a simplistic bike game prototype, then a month later decided to be developed into a full browser based games for PC. Develop throughout August and released in September
