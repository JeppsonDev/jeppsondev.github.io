Description

Wojon is a Swedish two man hobbyist studio founded by Jeppson and Willqvist, creating short and fun experiences for free.

History

Early history

Wojon was founded in 2016 as a highschool UF company, UF is an organization that helps young students create their own companies. One of the first games which Jeppson and Willqvist created was Blocktales, for a highschool project that had to be finished in 6 months. Later on they stopped making games for a couple of years, only to reconnect to create games such as The wiseman's clock, The unfaithfull stoyteller and The last pistachio.

Today

Today Wojon still creates games as a hobby for fun, recently finished Slopetron. A action endless bike racing game.
