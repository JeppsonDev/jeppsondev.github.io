Description

Wojon is a two-man hobbyist studio created by Jesper Byström and William Lundqvist. We create short and fun games, currently for free!

History

Early history

Wojon started out as a UF-Company in Sweden which is an organization that helps young people set up a company. Wojon stands for William och Jesper Online (william and jesper online)

After that

During studies Wojon was mostly inactive but has recently been used to publish awesome games such as our most recent title Slopetron!
